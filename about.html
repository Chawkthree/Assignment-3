Dan Mace

Dan typically shoots nature pictures and uploads new photos every month. Right now he's the only photographer that's featured on Stock Block.

Photographer Profile

I shoot all of my photos with a Nikon D3200 DSLR. All of the photos are shot in the RAW format and are edited in Photoshop and Lightroom before being published. Hundreds of photos are shot at every scene or event, but only a few are of high enough quality to be featured on Stock Block.

I really enjoy taking photos while I'm outside on walks or kayaking on ponds. I'm not as inspired by urbanized areas, but I do enjoy city skyline shots. Check out the photos!
